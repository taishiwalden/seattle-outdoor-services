Frequently Asked Questions

What services does your outdoor services company provide?
Our outdoor services company specializes in residential pressure washing, fence building, and deck repairs. We offer comprehensive pressure washing services to clean and restore various surfaces such as driveways, sidewalks, siding, and patios. Our team is skilled in building high-quality fences, including wood, vinyl, and chain-link options, tailored to meet your specific needs. We also provide deck repair and maintenance services, ensuring your outdoor space remains safe, functional, and visually appealing. You can view our price estimates on our pricing page here.
How can I request a quote for the services I need?
Requesting a quote is quick and easy! You can reach out to us through our website's contact form or give us a call at 206-245-7735. Please provide detailed information about the specific service(s) you require, such as the size of the area to be pressure washed, the type of fence you need, or the nature of your deck repair needs. The more details you provide, the better we can assess your requirements and provide an accurate quote. We strive to respond promptly to all inquiries and provide competitive pricing for our services.
Are your services available in my area?
We are proud to serve customers in Seattle, Shoreline, Lake Forest Park, West Seattle, Bothell, Mountlake Terrace, Bellevue, and Renton. Our outdoor services company operates in Seattle, Washington and surrounding areas. If you live in the area, but don't see your city listed, please don't hesitate to call!
